Privacy Policy

We keep privacy simple and strict: Magic Jester Match does not collect, store, or share personal data.

No collection
No storage
No sharing
No ads / trackers

1) Data collection

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, identifiers, location, contacts, photos, microphone).
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

2) Data storage

  • We do not operate a user data backend and do not store personal data on servers.
  • If the app stores anything locally (for example, game progress), it stays on the user’s device.

3) Data sharing

  • We do not sell or share user data with third parties.
