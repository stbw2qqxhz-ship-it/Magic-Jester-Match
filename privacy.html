Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Magic Jester Match handles data. We aim for clarity and transparency for users and App Review.

No data collection
No storage
No sharing
No analytics SDKs

Summary: We do not collect any personal data, we do not store it, and we do not transfer it to third parties.

What data we collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, identifiers, etc.).
  • We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV), location, contacts, photos, or microphone data.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

What data we store

  • We do not store personal data on our servers because we do not run any user data backend.
  • Any game progress, if present, remains on the user’s device only.

Sharing

  • We do not share or sell user data.
  • We do not transfer data to external partners.
